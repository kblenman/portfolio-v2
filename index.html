About Me

The Fun Stuff

Unfortunately, I can't start this about me section stating that I've always wanted to work in the tech industry or have been active in it since a very young age. In fact, I spent most of my younger years wanting to become a doctor or veterinarian! However, during my final year of high school, I began to truly realize that understanding technology was much more interesting and natural for me. From breaking open my PlayStation 3 in order to temporarily fix the dreaded "yellow light of death" to discussing with friends what changes Rockstar should make in the next Grand Theft Auto installment, it was all fun for me.

Realizing this, I decided to dedicate my college years to learning more about technology and haven't looked back since... okay, okay, at least not seriously. Although there is so much to be learned, I've enjoyed my journey so far and look foward to continuing in this wonderful field.

Of course, life isn't all about work. Thus, when I'm not working, I enjoy spending time with my family, gaming, or learning more about astrology and tarot.
